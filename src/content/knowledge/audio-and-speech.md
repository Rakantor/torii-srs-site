Audio helps reinforce correct reading and pronunciation.

## Audio sources

- Native audio for vocab and example sentences when available.
- Text-to-speech voices for Japanese and English.

## Playback controls

- Autoplay vocab audio or sentence audio.
- Play audio at the start of a review or after answering.
- Auto-play on incorrect answers to reinforce learning.
