Torii SRS includes a generous free plan and an optional Pro tier.

## Included for free

- Spaced repetition system.
- Offline mode.
- Self-study.
- Japanese and English audio.
- Pitch accent visualization.
- Cross-device syncing.

## Pro features

- Higher or unlimited custom vocabulary capacity.
- Larger notes limit.
- Custom decks.
