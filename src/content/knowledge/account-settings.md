Torii SRS includes settings to personalize how you study and how the app behaves on your devices.

## Account basics

- Change your username and email from settings.
- Reset your password using the email recovery flow.
- Sign in on multiple devices and keep progress synced when online.

## Study controls

- Set your timezone and 24-hour clock preference.
- Enable Vacation Mode to pause SRS scheduling while you are away.
- Toggle manual Offline Mode if you are preparing for no connectivity.

## Personalization

- Switch between light and dark themes.
- Customize theme colors, including pitch accent colors.
- Randomize Japanese fonts using your preferred font list.

## Reset and recovery

- Clear local data and re-download your synced content.
- Reset progress or delete your account from the Danger Zone section.
