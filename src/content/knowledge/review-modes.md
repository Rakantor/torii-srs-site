Reviews are flexible so you can train both recognition and production.

## Review directions

- JP to EN
- EN to JP
- JP to JP
- JP and EN mixed

## Review styles

- Text input with IME-style romaji to kana conversion.
- Quick Review flashcards with flip and thumbs up or down.

## Answer checking rules

- Synonyms and alternate meanings are accepted.
- Alternate readings are accepted.
- Minor typos are tolerated.
- You can mark an answer as different but correct.

## Session controls

- Undo the previous item.
- Skip or mark "I don't know".
- Enable Lightning Mode for auto-advance.
- Enable auto-submit while typing.
