Torii SRS is offline-first, so you can keep studying without a connection after your data syncs.

## What works offline

- Lessons, reviews, self-study, and browsing local vocabulary.
- Editing settings that are stored locally.

## What does not work offline

- Syncing with the server or other devices.
- Audio playback.
- Dictionary search and dictionary-based quick add.
- Contact form submissions.
- Account services such as changing email or password, signing out, or purchasing Pro.

## Best practices

- Sync while online before going offline.
- Keep the app open to finish any pending sync changes once you are back online.
