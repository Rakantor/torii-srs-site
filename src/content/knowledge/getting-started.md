Torii SRS is designed to help you build and retain Japanese vocabulary with a predictable daily flow. Start online so your data syncs, then study anywhere with offline-first support.

## Set up your account

- Create an account and sign in.
- Allow the initial sync to finish so your vocabulary data is ready.
- Choose your daily lesson limit and lesson batch size to match your schedule.

## Learn the daily flow

- Lessons introduce new words.
- Reviews reinforce words you have already learned.
- The dashboard shows what is due today and what is coming up next.

## Study online or offline

- Once your data has synced, you can keep studying without a connection.
- When you are back online, your progress syncs across devices.
