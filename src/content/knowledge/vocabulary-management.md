Torii SRS gives you full control over your vocabulary, including custom entries and bulk actions.

## Add vocabulary

- Add words manually with reading, meaning, and example sentences.
- Include part of speech, JLPT level, notes, and mnemonics.
- Choose the initial SRS stage for a new item.

## Dictionary search

- Search by English, Japanese, or romaji via Jisho.
- Tagged results show JLPT or WaniKani status when available.
- Quick add prevents duplicates.

## Browse and organize

- Filter lists by JLPT level, SRS stage, or review mode.
- View accuracy by review direction.
- Bulk actions include archive, prioritize, delete custom items, or downgrade stage.

## Item details

- See furigana, pitch accent, meanings, and example sentences.
- Add custom synonyms and readings.
- Jump to external references like Jisho or Tatoeba.
