Self-Study lets you practice without affecting your SRS progression.

## Self-Study mode

- Review learned, custom, or archived items.
- Filter by review direction and SRS stage.
- Enable Quick Review for fast flashcard sessions.

## Extra Study sessions

- Practice recent lessons or recent mistakes.
- Extra Study sessions do not change your SRS stage.
- Use Extra Study to reinforce weak areas before formal reviews.
