Lessons introduce new vocabulary in batches you control.

## Lesson queue

- Items are ordered by JLPT level by default, starting at N5.
- You can prioritize selected items to move them to the front.
- Archive items directly from lessons if you want to skip them.

## Lesson settings

- Daily lesson limit and lesson batch size are adjustable.
- Pitch accent display can be toggled on or off.
- Hide furigana or sentence meanings if you want more challenge.
- Autoplay audio for vocab and example sentences.
