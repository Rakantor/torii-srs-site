Torii SRS keeps you on track with a predictable daily routine. Lessons add new words, and reviews bring them back at the right time.

## Lessons

- Set a daily lesson limit to control how many new words you learn each day.
- Choose a lesson batch size so new words arrive in manageable groups.
- Lessons default to JLPT order (N5 to N1), so you learn foundational words first.

## Reviews

- Reviews are scheduled by SRS stage and show up when they are due.
- You can review in multiple modes, including Japanese to English and English to Japanese.
- Session summaries show what you got right and wrong by stage.

## Forecast and pacing

- The dashboard shows upcoming review counts so you can pace your day.
- Consistent daily reviews lead to faster progression through SRS stages.
