If you cannot sign in or sync, check the common causes below.

## Cookies and private browsing

- Cookies are required for authentication.
- Private browsing modes can block storage and prevent login.

## Offline sign-in

- If you have already synced data on a device, you can sign in offline.
- First-time sign-in requires a connection to download your data.

## Cross-device syncing

- Changes sync automatically when online.
- If you are offline, progress queues locally and syncs later.

## Language support

- The app supports only English for now.
